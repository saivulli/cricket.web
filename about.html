About Crixon

Crixon is your modern, all-in-one destination for cricket fans. We bring you the latest Indian and international cricket schedules, player stats, and a curated cricket shop—all in a beautiful, monochrome design.

Features

  • Upcoming Schedules: Stay updated with the latest fixtures, venues, and match times for Team India and major international tours.
  • Player Stats: Explore detailed stats, bios, and images for top Indian and international cricketers.
  • Interactive Search & Filters: Instantly find players by name, country, or role.
  • Cricket Shop: Discover and shop for the latest cricket gear and merchandise.
  • Responsive & Animated: Enjoy a seamless, animated experience on any device.

Our Vision

We believe cricket is more than a game—it's a passion that unites millions. Crixon aims to be the go-to platform for fans to follow matches, celebrate players, and connect with the sport they love.
